How It Works

1. Log in

Analyst signs in securely.

2. Data is scored

AI model scores real transaction + cyber telemetry data.

3. Risk is explained

Every flag comes with a plain-English reason, not a black box.

4. Analyst acts

Review alerts, monitor trends, take action.

Where The Data Comes From

Trained on real, publicly available datasets (Kaggle's Credit Card Fraud Detection dataset and a real Cybersecurity Intrusion Detection dataset) — not fake or randomly generated numbers. It does not connect to any live bank system; in production, the same model would take in a bank's real transaction and security log feeds instead.
