What's Inside The Dashboard

Dashboard

Your home screen — shows safe vs. suspicious vs. fraud counts at a glance, plus top AI-flagged transactions.

💳

Live Transaction Monitoring

Streams and scores individual transactions in real time, showing each one's risk score as it's evaluated.

🌐

Cybersecurity Telemetry

Shows login/session signals (failed logins, encryption used, session length) tied to each transaction.

🧠

AI Threat Detection

The model's verdict on each flagged transaction, plus a plain-English reason why it looked suspicious.

Fraud Analytics

Aggregate charts and trends — how much fraud, how severe, and how it's distributed across the batch.

How It Works

1. Log in

Analyst signs in securely.

2. Data is scored

AI model scores real transaction + cyber telemetry data.

3. Risk is explained

Every flag comes with a plain-English reason, not a black box.

4. Analyst acts

Review alerts, monitor trends, take action.

Where The Data Comes From

This prototype is trained on real, publicly available datasets (Kaggle's Credit Card Fraud Detection dataset and a real Cybersecurity Intrusion Detection dataset) — not fake or randomly generated numbers. It does not connect to any live bank system; in a production deployment, the same model would instead take in a bank's real transaction and security log feeds.
